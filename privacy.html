Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Puffberry Studio builds mobile games. This Privacy Policy explains how Puffberry Studio apps access, collect, use, and share information.

Developer

Developer name: Puffberry Studio

Contact email: puffberrystudio@gmail.com

Information collected by our apps

Puffberry Studio apps do not require users to create an account. Our apps do not ask users to provide their name, email address, phone number, address, or other directly identifiable personal information.

Our apps may store gameplay progress, game settings, unlocked content, coins, achievements, or similar game-related information locally on the user’s device.

Advertising and third-party services

Puffberry Studio apps may use Google AdMob to display ads, including banner ads and rewarded ads.

Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain information for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes, including IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, device identifiers, and advertising identifiers.

This information may be used by Google and its partners to provide, measure, personalize, and improve advertising services, prevent fraud, and maintain service security.

In-app purchases

Puffberry Studio apps may offer optional in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.

Purchases are processed by Google Play. Puffberry Studio does not collect or store payment card information.

How information is used

Information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve the game experience.
  • Save local game progress and preferences.
  • Show ads, including rewarded ads.
  • Support in-app purchases.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, or fraud.
  • Comply with applicable platform, legal, and policy requirements.

Data sharing

Puffberry Studio does not sell users’ personal information.

Some data may be collected or processed by third-party services used in our apps, including Google AdMob and Google Play services, according to their own privacy practices.

Data security

Data collected by third-party services may be transmitted using secure protocols. Puffberry Studio uses reasonable measures to protect information related to its apps, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data retention and deletion

Game progress and preferences stored locally on the device may be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing the app’s data from the device settings.

Puffberry Studio does not create user accounts inside its apps. If users have privacy questions or deletion requests related to information handled by Puffberry Studio, they may contact us at puffberrystudio@gmail.com.

For data collected by Google services, users may manage advertising preferences and privacy controls through their Google account and Android device settings.

Children’s privacy

Puffberry Studio apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If Puffberry Studio becomes aware that personal information from a child has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Puffberry Studio may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: puffberrystudio@gmail.com
